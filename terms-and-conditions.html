1. Purpose and Scope

This Team Data Policy establishes how ResourceMojo Business Outsourcing Pvt. Ltd. (“ResourceMojo”, “RecruFlex”, “we”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects data relating to:

  • Employees
  • Consultants
  • Contract recruiters
  • Remote team members

The Policy applies to all internal data collected or processed during the course of employment, engagement, or association with ResourceMojo. It applies globally to all jurisdictions where our teams operate, including India 🇮🇳,the EU 🇪🇺, Canada 🇨🇦, and the US 🇺🇸.

2. Categories of Data Collected

We collect the following categories of data about team members:

2.1 Personal Identification Data

  • Full name, date of birth, gender, contact details, emergency contact
  • Government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, SSN/SIN for overseas hires)

2.2 Employment & Professional Data

  • Offer letters, employment agreements, role details
  • Payroll, tax records, bank account details
  • Performance appraisals, feedback, promotions, disciplinary records

2.3 Background & Verification Data

  • BGV reports, reference checks, academic certifications
  • Police verification, if applicable

2.4 IT & Access Logs

  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, email system usage
  • ATS (Applicant Tracking System) access history
  • Timesheet entries, leave records, login/logout timestamps

2.5 Special Categories (Where Applicable)

  • Health insurance information (for benefits)
  • Diversity & inclusion data (voluntarily shared)
  • Immigration/visa documents for overseas employees

3.Sources of Data

We collect data from the following sources:

  • Directly from employees during onboarding and through HR systems
  • Automatically from company IT systems (email, ATS, time tracking)
  • Third-party vendors (e.g. BGV agencies, payroll processors)
  • Government authorities for compliance (tax filings, PF, ESIC, etc.)

4. Purpose of Data Collection & Use

We collect and process team data for legitimate HR and operational purposes, including:

Recruitment & onboarding Verifying candidate credentials,issuing Offers Consent/ Contract
Payroll & Benefits Processing salaries ,taxes, insurance Legal Obligation / Contract

5. Access Control and Data Security

We implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to ensure only authorized personnel can access team data.

  • HR staff have controlled access to employment & payroll data.
  • Tech administrators access IT system logs only for operational/security purposes.
  • Background verification data is restricted to compliance & HR.
  • Every access is logged and monitored.

Security measures include:

  • Encryption of stored and transmitted data
  • Two-factor authentication for sensitive systems
  • Regular access audits and revocation for exiting staff
  • Confidentiality agreements for all employees and vendors

6. Cross-Border Data Transfers

As RecruFlex works with distributed teams and global clients, employee data may be transferred to or processed in other jurisdictions (e.g., payroll processing, client project access, remote hiring).

We ensure:

  • Transfers comply with Indian IT Rules, GDPR (using Standard Contractual Clauses), PIPEDA adequacy rules, and CCPA where applicable.
  • Transfers are logged and documented.
  • Employees are informed when their data is processed overseas

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain team data only for as long as required by law or legitimate business purposes.
