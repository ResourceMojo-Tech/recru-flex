About Us

RecruFlex was born out of a simple vision: to simplify hiring for startups and growing businesses while creating opportunities for untapped talent pools beyond major metros. Built in-house by the ResourceMojo team, RecruFlex combines technology, automation, and human expertise to make recruitment seamless.
